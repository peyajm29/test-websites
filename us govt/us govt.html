US Federal Government Accessibility Campaign

Helping to promote equal access to information and services for all.

Our Mission

We strive to improve accessibility to government resources and services. Our new campaign focuses on raising awareness and implementing solutions to ensure equal access to the disabled community.

Features of the Campaign:

  • Advocacy for accessible website design and development
  • Educational resources and guidelines for compliance
  • Partnerships with organizations supporting accessibility initiatives
  • Regular accessibility audits of government websites
  • Collaboration with disability communities to address their needs
  • And much more!
